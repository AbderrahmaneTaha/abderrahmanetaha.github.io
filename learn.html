NGNR OS

"A modern, lightweight operating system based on Arch Linux designed specifically for Engineers"

What can NGNR OS offer you?

Privacy:

We believe in user privacy, and we prioritize it above everything. NGNR OS does not collect or store any info from the users and it will stay like this forever.

Open Source:

NGNR OS is and always will be open source and free to use because we value our users, and we'll always try to keep them engaged and involved with our product.

Full Control:

We want our users to have full control on their hardware and software unlike proprietary operating systems practices. NGNR OS will also be highly customizable to the liking of the user!

Out Of The Box:

Everything just works "Out Of The Box", no need to tweak or configure anything and there's no need for unnecessary questions.

Install what you want:

With NGNR OS you can run many native Windows applications using compatibility software such as Wine, PlayonLinux or Proton, via Steam or Lutris and GameHub, and even a lot more to explore!

Designed for Engineers:
