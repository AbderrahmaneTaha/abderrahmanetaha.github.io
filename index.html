NGNR OS

Fast, Free and Friendly Operating System curated for Engineers.

NGNR OS is an open-source, secure and user friendly operating system desigend for STEM and power users that are looking for a modern, swift and elegant Linux experience ready out of the box.

With all the software that you will need ready from the get go you can now discover your work station's full potential and how you can optimize and refine your workflow with NGNR OS today!

Next

Engineering Oriented

No need to hustle with downloading any software or tool, NGNR OS already has covered that for you.

With prepackaged industry standard programs whether paid like Matlab or open source like Octave and Scilab.

And just to name few: AutoCAD Electrical, Xilinx, Proteus, Free DFM, LTspice, PCB Artist, LabWindows are all included and more!
